Copyright Issue

We hold the Intellectual Property Rights (IPR) for many games on Gamely Hub's sites. For certain games, we have obtained licences from known copyright holders. However, for some games, we could not locate copyright information, or it is no longer available due to widespread internet use.

Some games are used under specific conditions, which Gamely Hub has met to the best of our knowledge.

We do not alter the source code of the games, ensuring that credits, brand names, and website references remain intact. If you believe your copyright or other IPR is being infringed on Gamely Hub's sites, please notify us immediately with the following information:

  1. The electronic or physical signature of the IPR owner or an authorised representative.
  2. A description of the IPR that has been infringed and the infringing activity.
  3. The location of the unauthorised copy (e.g., the URL on Gamely Hub's website).
  4. A copy of the licence granting you the right to use and protect the IPR (if you are not the owner).
  5. Your name and full contact details.
  6. A statement affirming your sincere belief that the disputed use is unauthorised.

Send the above details to app@gamelyhub.com. We will review your submission and take action as soon as possible, responding to any queries within 7 days.
